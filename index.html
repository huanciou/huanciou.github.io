Sheldon Lee Cooper Ph.D., Sc.D.,is a fictional character in the CBS television series The Big Bang Theory
He has a genius-level IQ of 187 but displays a fundamental lack of social skills, a tenuous understanding of humor, and difficulty recognizing irony and sarcasm in other people, although he himself often employs them. He exhibits highly idiosyncratic behavior and a general lack of humility, empathy, and toleration.
These characteristics provide the majority of the humor involving him, which has caused him to be described as the show's breakout character.
Some viewers have asserted that Sheldon's personality is consistent with Asperger syndrome and obsessive–compulsive disorder
